About

This project is just a proof of concept for a portal, where users can freely place their small jobs to be done, while others can participate and bid their prices, for which they are able to perform proposed task.
The idea came to my friend's mind while he was watching TV, where truck drivers were competing against each other to transport goods across country.
And because while being teenager, I was looking for place to find some job. It was hard at times, many portals, not many real offers and opportunities.
